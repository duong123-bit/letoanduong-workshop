# Event Report: “Saturday Meetup”
### Objectives of Participation
- Network at the Saturday Meetup organized by the AWS Study Group community.

- Gain practical insights and real-world perspectives from the speakers and guest experts.

- Connect with IT industry veterans, seniors, and fellow tech students.

- Accumulate hands-on experience to apply directly to academic learning and software project development.

### Event Highlights
- Hackathon Real-World Experience: Valuable insights from a Hackathon alumnus covering the end-to-end journey—from ideation and team collaboration to rapid product development and key lessons learned post-competition.

- "Dai Viet Tu Vi" Project Case Study: A technical deep-dive into product initialization, system architecture design, and the actual challenges faced when deploying an application to a production environment.

- Open Discussion & Networking: A collaborative QA session with guest speakers focusing on software development life cycle optimization, Cloud Computing trends, and future career paths.

### Key Takeaways
- Product Mindset: Understood the roadmap required to materialize a technology solution, moving from initial concept definition to final market release.

- Technical Architecture: Absorbed foundational architectural thinking and practical troubleshooting methods for web application operations from senior industry perspectives.

- Soft Skills: Recognized the value of fast-paced environments like Hackathons in honing risk-management reflexes and accelerating teamwork dynamics under tight deadlines.

### Application to Learning and Work
- Standardize the MVP Process: Apply lean product design (Minimum Viable Product) and strict timeline management to both major academic assignments and personal projects.

- Optimize Infrastructure Design: Implement architectural concepts and system optimization strategies learned from the case studies into the layout of future web applications.

- Advance Cloud Skills: Proactively dive deeper into the AWS ecosystem and Cloud technologies to prepare for upcoming tech competitions and large-scale deployments.

### Personal Experience & Reflections
- The Saturday Meetup provided highly intuitive perspectives that successfully bridged the gap between university theory and the real-world tech landscape, thanks to the stories shared by experienced guests.

- The Hackathon panel was deeply inspiring, offering concrete lessons on lean product optimization, team management, and how to adapt flexibly to critical issues right before deadlines. Meanwhile, the real-world journey of the "Dai Viet Tu Vi" project helped me clearly visualize the stages of design, infrastructure deployment, and maintaining web system stability under production workloads.

- Beyond technical knowledge, the event served as a powerful bridge to integrate into the tech community, consult industry seniors, and connect with like-minded peers who share the same career direction in Cloud Computing and Software Engineering.
